Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of 200 tech enthusiasts in San Francisco. What started as a local meetup has grown into one of the world's premier technology conferences, attracting over 10,000 attendees annually from across the globe.

Over the years, TechCon has evolved from focusing primarily on web development to encompassing artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, blockchain, and emerging technologies that shape our digital future.

Our Mission

Our mission is to foster innovation and collaboration within the global technology community. We believe in creating spaces where ideas can flourish, knowledge can be shared, and the next generation of tech leaders can emerge.

TechCon is committed to diversity, accessibility, and sustainability. We strive to make technology education available to all and to create conferences that are inclusive, environmentally conscious, and forward-thinking.

Notable Past Speakers

Dr. Sarah Chen

Director of AI Research at NeuroTech, Dr. Chen pioneered breakthroughs in neural network optimization. Her 2022 keynote on "Ethical AI Development" set new standards for responsible artificial intelligence implementation across the industry.

Marcus Johnson

Founder of SecureNet, Marcus is a renowned cybersecurity expert who has helped Fortune 500 companies protect against sophisticated cyber threats. His 2021 workshop on "Zero Trust Architecture" revolutionized corporate security protocols.

Priya Patel

As CTO of ChainSolutions, Priya has been at the forefront of blockchain innovation. Her 2023 presentation on "Sustainable Blockchain Solutions" introduced energy-efficient consensus mechanisms that reduced carbon footprint by 70%.

David Kim

Creator of the popular React framework extension, David has shaped modern web development practices. His 2020 talk on "The Future of Frontend Architecture" influenced how developers build scalable web applications today.
